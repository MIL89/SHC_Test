Altar Angels

The Sacred Heart Altar Angels is a dedicated group consisting of 18 members who play an essential role in the church's liturgical celebrations, particularly during holy masses. These individuals are committed to serving at the altar and actively participate in various activities that contribute to the spiritual and social life of the church community. As altar servers, their primary responsibility is to assist the priests and deacons during the liturgy. They assist in setting up the holy altar, carrying liturgical items, such as the crucifix, candles, and the book of prayers. They also assist during the Eucharistic celebration, ensuring the seamless flow of the ceremony and supporting the clergy in their duties. However, the role of the Sacred Heart Altar Servers goes beyond serving during mass. They actively engage in other group activities that contribute to the spiritual enrichment of the community. For example, they participate in crib making, a traditional practice where they create a nativity scene inside the church during the Christmas season. This activity helps to visually represent the birth of Jesus and brings the community together in celebration.

Additionally, on Good Friday, the altar servers contribute to the commemoration of the crucifixion by preparing a depiction of Mount Calvary. This powerful visual representation aids in contemplation and reflection on the sacrifice of Jesus Christ. Through these group activities, the members of the Sacred Heart Altar Servers play a vital role in enhancing the spiritual atmosphere of the church and fostering a sense of unity among the congregation. Being an altar server is not only a service but also an opportunity for personal growth and development. By actively participating in liturgical celebrations and engaging in various activities, the members have the chance to deepen their spiritual understanding and connection with their faith. They learn the importance of reverence, discipline, and attentiveness while serving at the altar. These experiences help to cultivate their spiritual lives and provide a solid foundation for their future as individuals committed to a spiritual path.

Moreover, the group activities and interactions within the Sacred Heart Altar Servers also foster social skills and promote a sense of camaraderie. By working together as a team, the members develop cooperation, communication, and leadership skills. They learn to support and rely on one another, creating strong bonds of friendship within the group. In conclusion, the Sacred Heart Altar Servers are a dedicated group of individuals who actively participate in the church's liturgical celebrations and engage in various activities to enhance the spiritual and social life of the community. Through their service at the altar and involvement in group activities, they not only contribute to the smooth conduct of religious ceremonies but also develop their spiritual and social skills, preparing them for a fulfilling spiritual life in the future.
