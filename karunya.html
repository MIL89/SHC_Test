Karunya Trust



Karunya Trust considers the roles of a facilitator and implementer as its primary functions. These roles involve actively partnering with various stakeholders for the development of society, with a special focus on children. The Trust aims to provide a safety net for thousands of vulnerable children who lack basic necessities and opportunities for growth and development. Regardless of caste, creed, and religion, Karunya Trust caters to the needs of needy children, ensuring that no child is left behind. One of the key objectives is to design and implement new programs that contribute to the overall development of children, encompassing educational, vocational, and health facilities.

Moreover, the trust places significant emphasis on the financial well-being of families by offering vocational training and income generation programs. These efforts are directed at empowering families to improve their economic status and provide a stable environment for their children's growth. All activities undertaken by Karunya Trust are inspired by the model established by Mother Teresa, and the Trust is dedicated to her memory. We receive multiple requests for help, ranging from education to m.edical assistance. To ensure the legitimacy of these requests, we personally visit the homes of the requestors. During these visits, we carefully assess the genuineness of their situations and compile detailed reports.

These reports are then subjected to thorough analysis, taking into account the severity of each case and the specific assistance required. For financial aid related to education, we issue cheques directly in the name of the schools or organizations involved. This ensures that the support reaches the intended beneficiaries, enabling them to access the educational resources they need to improve their lives and future prospects. Our commitment to verifying and channeling help responsibly allows us to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those who genuinely need it. The dedication to Mother Teresa's legacy underscores the Trust's commitment to serving society and caring for the most vulnerable members, particularly children.

Events Conducted


Relocating destitutes from the road to various aashrams provides them with shelter and a supportive community. This initiative aims to offer these vulnerable individuals a chance at a more dignified and secure life, fostering their physical and spiritual well-being.

Relocation

During COVID relief efforts, the distribution of food kits played a vital role in supporting those in need. These kits, comprising essential non-perishable food items like rice, grains provided a lifeline to vulnerable individuals and families.

Covid Relief

During the COVID-19 pandemic, donating sanitizers became an essential act of kindness to aid society. The provision of sanitizers played a crucial role in promoting hygiene awareness and safeguarding public health, exemplifying the spirit of collective responsibility during challenging times.
