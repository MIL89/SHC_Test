The Church celebrateed the second World Day for Grandparents and the Elderly. Established by Pope Francis the observance is celebrated each year on the fourth Sunday of July, coinciding with the feasts of the Blessed Virgin Mary’s parents and Jesus’ grandparents, Sts. Joachim and Anne.

“Old age is no time to give up and lower the sails, but a season of enduring fruitfulness. ‘The special sensibility that those of us who are elderly have for the concerns, thoughts and the affections that make us human should once again become the vocation of many. It would be a sign of our love for the younger generations’”
