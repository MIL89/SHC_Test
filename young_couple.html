Young Couple

An organization which was formed with the aim of providing opportunities for newly married couples to actively participate in activities conducted by the Church. This initiative recognizes the importance of nurturing and involving couples in the life of the Church as they embark on their journey of married life. The organization consists of approximately 40 members who share a common desire to deepen their spiritual connection as a couple and contribute to the community.


By joining this organization, newly married couples have the chance to engage in various activities and events organized by the Church. These activities may include retreats, workshops, marriage enrichment programs, social gatherings, and volunteer opportunities. Through their participation, couples can strengthen their bond, seek guidance and support from experienced couples, and build lasting friendships within the Church community. The organization serves as a platform for couples to share their experiences, challenges, and joys with others who are in a similar life stage. It creates a supportive environment where couples can seek advice, learn from one another, and grow together in faith. Additionally, being part of this group allows couples to contribute their talents and skills to the Church, serving as active members in various ministries and outreach initiatives.


Overall, this organization plays a crucial role in fostering a sense of belonging and involvement for newly married couples within the Church. It provides a space for them to deepen their spiritual connection, build strong relationships, and actively contribute to the growth and well-being of the Church community.
