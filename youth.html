In the late 90's, a journey of youth began at Sacred Heart Church, symbolized by a small seed that represented adolescence. Over the years, this seed has grown into a mighty tree, thanks to the unwavering dedication, coordination, determination, inspiration, and prayers of the youth at Sacred Heart Church. The Sacred Heart Youth Bhayander (SHYBS) has become a beacon of strength and support, not only for our parish or forane but also at the diocese level. The youth of Sacred Heart Church have worked tirelessly to nurture and develop this tree, which now extends its shade and influence across the entire Kalyan diocese.

Through their active involvement in various activities, events, and initiatives, the youth of Sacred Heart Church have proven themselves to be the epitome of excellence. Their commitment to faith, community, and service has earned them the reputation of being the best youth group in the entire Kalyan diocese.






Our Venture

2006-2007
Formation Of the Name SHYBS

Since its establishment in 2006, SHYBS has achieved numerous accolades, including 1st Prize in Talentia (Diocese), 1st Prize in Dance and Comedy Skit (Borivali zone), 2nd Prize in Crib, Fancy Dress & Street Play Competition - Borivali Zone, and 2nd Prize in Faith Expo.

2007-2008
Remarkable milestones

SHYBS achieved remarkable milestones, being awarded as the Best Youth of Kalyan diocese, securing 1st prize in the comedy and dance competition in the Borivali Zone, winning 2nd prize in the Zonal Crib Competition, and unveiling its first-ever logo

2008-2009
Triumph

SHYBS achieved remarkable accomplishments, being awarded as the Best Youth of Kalyan Diocese and securing first prizes in various categories. They excelled in Borivali Zone sports, winning first place. Additionally, they clinched the first prize in cricket (boys), badminton (girls), tug of war, and the Mira-Bhayandar Youth competition.

2010-2011
Active Engagements

SHYBS actively engages in various activities, including blood donation drives, updating their logo, and organizing Yuvanam events.

2012-2013
Participations

SHYBS organizes the KEY Marathon and actively participates in blood donation drives.

2014-2015
Exciting Events

SHYBS organizes exciting events such as Cyclothon and Yuvanam, bringing together youth and promoting physical fitness and vibrant community engagement.

2016-2017
Various Happenings

SHYBS undertook diverse initiatives, such as organizing one-time meal events, securing 1st place in a biblical skit competition, achieving 3rd place in break-through , excelling in diocesan sports with 1st place in relay and 3rd place in long jump, 200m, and 400m events. They also visited an orphanage, participated in Yuvanam activities, and performed a street play to raise social awareness about water conservation.

2018-2019
Numerous impactful initiatives

SHYBS has been involved in numerous impactful initiatives, including organizing one-time meals, conducting fund-raising events for Kerala flood relief, leading newspaper drives, organizing Cycloton events, hosting seminars, participating in wall painting projects, organizing Yuvanam events, providing 25-day food items for orphanages, and achieving the 3rd prize in Break Thru competitions.

2020-2021
Super Mario 64

SHYBS was recognized as the Best Youth of Kalyan Diocese and achieved remarkable victories, including the first prize in sports in the Borivali Zone, first prize in cricket (boys), first prize in badminton (girls), first prize in tug of war, and first prize in the Mira-Bhayandar Youth competition.
