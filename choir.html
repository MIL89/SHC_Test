Choir

  1. Home
  2. Choir

Sacred Hearts Church Choir was established under the guidance of Fr. Pothenparambil since the inception of our church. From the very beginning, the choir has been enthusiastic and active in leading the devotees, ensuring their active participation in the Holy Mass. They are always available for church events and activities, going the extra mile to serve the Lord. Although many active church members who were part of the choir have shifted, they are still remembered fondly in our hearts.


During the tenure of Fr. Joemon Kurisingal, the choir was renamed as Angel's Voice Choir, and Mr. Christus was chosen as the first official choir master. Angel's Voice Choir actively engages in bringing children closer to Christ by helping them learn about music and instruments. They provide a platform where children feel a deep connection with Jesus. The choir of Angel's Voice performs on special occasions such as Christmas, the Holy Week, feasts, liturgically important days, days of abstinence, and Remembrance Days of saints. Additionally, the Sunday choir is divided into four parts, offering opportunities for the youth and catechism students to grow and experience Jesus. Overall, Sacred Hearts Church Choir, now known as Angel's Voice Choir, has played a vital role in our church community, guiding and inspiring devotees to actively participate in worship and fostering a deeper connection with Jesus through music.


The choir schedule at our church is organized in a way that allows different groups to take part in leading the congregation in worship on specific Sundays. On the first Sunday of each month, we have the Catechism choir, which comprises the young students from our religious education program. They bring their youthful energy and enthusiasm to the service, showcasing their growing musical abilities. The second Sunday features the Senior Choir, a group of experienced singers who have been part of our church community for many years. Their voices blend harmoniously as they lift their voices in praise and worship, providing a sense of maturity and depth to the liturgy.


On the third Sunday, it's time for the Youth Choir to take the stage. This choir consists of our talented young members, offering them a chance to showcase their musical skills and passion for worship. They bring a vibrant and youthful spirit to the Mass, connecting with their peers and inspiring others through their joyful singing. Finally, on the fourth Sunday of the month, we have the Main Choir leading the congregation in worship. This choir comprises dedicated members from various age groups, combining their voices to create a powerful and uplifting musical experience. They provide a solid foundation for the congregation's participation and create a sense of unity and reverence during the Holy Mass. By dividing the Sundays into different choir groups, we offer opportunities for each segment of our church community to actively engage in worship and express their love for God through music. This arrangement ensures a diverse and enriching musical experience for everyone who attends our services.
