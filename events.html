St. Alphonsa's feast celebration is a vibrant and joyous event that commemorates the life and legacy of Saint Alphonsa, the first Indian woman to be canonized as a saint by the Catholic Church. Devotees come together to participate in special Masses.

St Alphonsa Feast

The "JaaGo" yatra, an intercessory journey with the relic of St. John Paul II, took place at Sacred Heart Church, accompanied by a session of adoration. Jesus Youth India brought a 3cm fragment of a wooden holy cross that was held by St. John Paul II during his final days.

JaaGo

The St. Thomas feast celebration was a joyous occasion that commemorated the life and teachings of St. Thomas the Apostle. It involves religious ceremonies, special Masses, and processions in honor of the saint. Devotees come together to celebrate their faith and seek blessings.

St Thomas Feast

Sacred Heart Church Bhayandar joyfully celebrated the Feast of Sr. Vimal Maria. The festivities included religious ceremonies, Masses, and special prayers in honor of Sr. Vimal Maria. Devotees gathered to express their devotion and gratitude towards her on this significant occasion.

Sr Vimal Maria Feast

The Sacred Heart Feast celebration was marked by a Holy Mass presided over by Rev. Fr. Binoy. Devotees gathered to participate in the solemn ceremony and honor the Sacred Heart. The occasion was filled with prayers, worship, and a sense of reverence.

Sacred Heart Feast

The Sacred Heart Church celebrated Ash Monday in solemn remembrance of human mortality and need for reconciliation with God and marked the beginning of the Lent of Repentance by offering Holy Qurbana led by Parish Priest Fr. Binoy.
