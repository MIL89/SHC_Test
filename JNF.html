Jesus Nurse Fraternity (JNF)

To fulfill our mission of serving the sick and needy, JNF have undertaken several initiatives. Firstly, the active reach out to those who are unwell and require assistance. Our dedicated team ensures that the team provides them with the necessary support, including medical care, emotional support, and access to resources.


In addition, we firmly believe in the importance of preventive healthcare, especially for individuals with limited financial means. To address this, various awareness campaigns are conducted to educate the public about affordable health check-up packages. By spreading awareness, the aim is to empower individuals to prioritize their health and take advantage of accessible healthcare options. Recognizing the specific healthcare needs of industrial laborers, we have established a clinic tailored to cater to their requirements. This specialized clinic provides comprehensive medical services, focusing on the health concerns commonly faced by this demographic. We strive to make quality healthcare easily accessible and convenient for the industrious workforce.


Furthermore, there are regularly organize blood donation camps and free medical check-up camps. These camps serve as a platform for individuals to contribute to the noble cause of saving lives through blood donations. Simultaneously, we provide free medical check-ups to help identify potential health issues early on and offer appropriate guidance and treatment. Through these initiatives, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of the sick, raise awareness about affordable healthcare options, and provide necessary medical support to those in need. By joining hands with compassionate individuals and organizations, we can create a healthier and more caring community for all
